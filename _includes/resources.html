[1] Dorina Baltag, PhD: workshops for female researchers shifting to industry

[2] Veronika Cheplygina, PhD: workshops in open science and community building

[3] Felienne Hermans, PhD: workshops in leadership skills and social media management

International professional organizations which you may join as an Early Career Researcher:

[1] BeNeLux & France Local Chapter of the Controlled Release Society (you can meet with the organization representatives at one of the incoming events)

[2] eLife Ambassadors community (operating online)

[3] Science Innovation Union (operating mostly online)

[4] Science Meets Business (Leiden, the Netherlands)

Free online mentoring programs:

[1] Organization for Human Brain Mapping International Online Mentoring Programme

[2] Open Post Academic Mentorship Program incubated through the Mozilla Open Leaders program

National career development events dedicated to PhDs:

[1] The PhD Day in Groningen, over 700 PhDs in all disciplines, mid-September each year

[2] ICT.Open in Hilversum, over 500 ECRs, mid-March each year
