News

General news:

August 10th, 2019: Stichting Solaris receives the ANBI status.

ANBI is the official status of the organization for public good in the Netherlands. Congratulations to everyone involved and thank you for your hard work!

July 19th, 2019: Natalia Bielczyk and Stephan Heunis received a prestigious Science Grant from Mozilla Foundation. The grant is dedicated to organizing an intense 5-day event in Lorentz Centre, Leiden, October 28th - November 1st 2019. The bootcamp will be a cycle of workshops & open discussions on inclusivity, self-care, self-management, mentoring, open science & other topics. The Mozilla Foundation is partnering and will be present to listen about problems of today science and brainstorm solutions with us. Stichting Solaris is the receiving organization and we will manage the funds for the bootcamp.

July 1st, 2019: Ali Khatibi from University of Birmingham joins the Advisory Board of Stichting Solaris.

Ali is a senior researcher is the field of pain and neuroplasticity. He is also actively involved in a range of initiatives increasing awareness about bullying at work and in academic environments. Welcome Ali!

June 20th, 2019: Adeel Razi from Monash University, Melbourne joins the Advisory Board of Stichting Solaris.

Adeel originally came to academia from industry, where he worked in engineering for many years. Now he is an acknowledged researcher in connectomics, a branch of neuroscience. He is very conscious of similarities and differences between academia and industry. Welcome Adeel!

June 19th, 2019: Mozhan Soltani from Leiden University joins the Advisory Board of Stichting Solaris.

Mozhan is an expert in promoting Equality and Diversity in academia. Welcome Mozhan!

May 28th, 2019: Daniel Borek from Ghent University joins the Advisory Board of Stichting Solaris.

Daniel is an expert in Open Science practices and in disseminating science through social media. Welcome Daniel!

April 15th, 2019: Natalia Bielczyk was appointed as an eLife Associate 2019-2020

Natalia will help the community of eLife Ambassadors by igniting the discussion at the eLife Ambassadors Discourse forum, and giving a workhop on self development to the eLife Ambassadors.
